THE TAG JOURNEY SO FAR

2015, April
KAKUMA PROJECT

Uniting teachers offering a free education to African refugees

We shipped our laptops to Kakuma refugee camp. We went over there and set up power supply and internet connection. Then we united 325 teachers across 45 countries who started to teach refugees English, Math and Science, for free.
 

Learn More

2017, October
TeachSDGs + CLIMATE ACTION

1M+ Students, 100 Countries
1 Mission

We united teachers willing to focus on the UN SDG's in education and launched the #TeachSDGs community with 40,000 teachers on board. Our Climate Action Project was covered by BBC, CNN and National Geographic and endorsed by Jane Goodall.

Learn More

2019, January
INNOVATION LAB SCHOOL

Network of educators improving education for students in Africa

We developed a curriculum called the Global Goals Book, created by 16 authors
globally and translated to 10 languages. Our Lab Schools are in Palestine, Kenya and Tanzania and were featured on Netflix and Jane Goodall's documentary "The Hope" on National Geographic.

Learn More

2019, September
THE GOALS PROJECT

Classrooms from over 100 countries join to tackle the UN Global Goals

Teachers and students, united in purpose, joined in global teams to examine the UN SDG's and create solutions through innovation, invention, storytelling, and action. Students addressed challenges such as poverty, hunger, gender equality, and quality education for all.
